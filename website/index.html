Welcome to [IN]FINITE

Concordia University Design Show of 2020.

You are here as an early player.

Nice to meet you

The Department of Design and Computation Arts of Concordia University is excited to present their student-run year-end exhibition: [IN]FINITE.
This occasion allows undergraduate students to showcase a variety of artworks completed during their curriculum, from digital experiences, printed works, to designed objects.
Visitors will have a chance to glance at the strenuous design process behind the exhibited works by exploring premeditative sketches, prototypes and trains of thought.
The design process is never finite. Rather, it leads to infinite possibilities.

Join us from April 20th to April 25th at the Antheism Gallery, Mile-Ex neigborhood.

In the meantime, feel free to explore past-year shows and follow us on social media.
